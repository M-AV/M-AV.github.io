 - **60 ml** Scotch Whisky
 - **75 ml** Orange Juice
 - **15 ml** Lemon Juice
 - **5 ml** Orgeat Syrup
 - **5 ml** Syrup
 
 **Process**: Shake with ice and strain into glass. 

Made this with a Highland Park 12 Years and it's absolutely delicious. Was very surprised with this one and will definitely make it again one day.
