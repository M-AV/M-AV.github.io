 - **10-12** Mint leaves
 - **60 ml** Gin
 - **22.5 ml** Lemon Juice
 - **22.5 ml** Simple Syrup
 **Process**: Shake with ice and strain into glass
 **Garnish**: A single mint leave

 This is just a Gin sour with mint. It's delicious. This ratio is from Liquid Intelligence (p.133).
