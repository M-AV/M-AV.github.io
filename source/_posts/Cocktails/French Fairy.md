 - **45 ml** Cognac
 - **15 ml** St. Germain Elderflower Liqueur
 - **15 ml** Lemon Juice
 - **7.5 ml** Simple Sirup
 - **~2-3 ml** Absinthe

**Process**: Rinse glass with Absinthe. Shake remaining ingredients with ice, strain into glass.
