 - **60 ml** Light Rum
 - **30 ml** Lime Juice
 - **15 ml** Grenadine Syrup
 - **1** Egg white
 **Garnish**: Orange slice and Maraschino Cherry
 **Process**: Reverse dry shake

Like most other sours, but with a grenadine twist. Sweet. Easy to drink.
