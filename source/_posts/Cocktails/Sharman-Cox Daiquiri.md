 - **6 cl** White Rum
 - **1 cl** Blue Curacao
 - **0.75 cl** Lime Juice
 - **0.75 cl** Lemon Juice
 - **1 cl** Sugar Syrup
 - **1** Egg White

 **Process**: Shake all ingredients

Easy to drink. Somewhat sweet. Good looking. Mine was WAY more blue than most pictures I have seen of the same drink..
