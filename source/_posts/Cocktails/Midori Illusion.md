 - 60 ml Midori
 - 15 ml Triple Sec
 - 15 ml Vodka
 - 30 ml Lemon Juice
 - 30 ml Pineapple Juice

Shake ingredients and serve over ice.

It should be possible to get a better foam from the pineapple juice.
