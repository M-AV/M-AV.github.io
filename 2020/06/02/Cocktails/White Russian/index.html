White Russian

2020-06-02
Cocktails
  • 4 cl Vodka
  • 4 cl Kahlua
  • 4 cl Cream
  • Nutmeg for Garnish

Mix Vodka + Kahlua in glass with ice. Layer cream on top to taste.

Idea: Shake 1.5-2cl Amaretto with cream
Idea: Reduce amount of cream (see picture)
