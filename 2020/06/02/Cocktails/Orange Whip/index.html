Orange Whip

2020-06-02
Cocktails

Source: H2D

  • 12 cl Orange Juice
  • 3 cl Light Rum
  • 3 cl Vodka
  • 6 cl Cream
  • 1.5 cl Triple Sec

Can be blended or shaken with crushed ice.
